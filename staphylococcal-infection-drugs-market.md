<p><p>The Staphylococcal Infection Drugs Market is categorized into several types, including beta-lactams, peptides, quinolones, and others. Quinolones, like ciprofloxacin, inhibit bacterial DNA replication. The "others" category includes alternative treatments like glycopeptides and oxazolidinones, which are effective against resistant strains. Each type plays a crucial role in combating Staphylococcal infections, particularly in an era of increasing antibiotic resistance.</p></p>
