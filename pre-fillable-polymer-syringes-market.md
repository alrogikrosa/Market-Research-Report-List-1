<p><p>The Pre-Fillable Polymer Syringes market is primarily segmented into two types: Cyclic Olefin Polymer (COP) and Cyclic Olefin Copolymer (COC). COP offers excellent clarity, low moisture permeability, and high chemical resistance, making it ideal for stable drug delivery. In contrast, COC combines the benefits of both polymers, providing enhanced mechanical properties and improved gas barrier performance. Both types cater to the growing demand for safe, efficient, and user-friendly drug administration in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
