<p><p>Dual Plate Check Valves are specialized devices designed to prevent backflow in piping systems. <p><p>Dual plate check valves are crucial components in fluid systems, allowing backflow prevention. The wafer type features a slim design, sandwiched between flanges, suitable for tight spaces. It offers lightweight benefits and easy installation. In contrast, the lug type has threaded holes for bolting, affording easy removal without needing to drain the pipeline. This type is advantageous in maintenance-heavy applications where valve access is frequent. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing operational efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
