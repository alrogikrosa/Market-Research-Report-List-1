<p><p>The animal vaccination market comprises various types including live attenuated vaccines, inactivated vaccines, and other vaccine types. Live attenuated vaccines use weakened pathogens to stimulate an immune response without causing disease, offering long-lasting immunity. Inactivated vaccines contain killed pathogens, providing safer alternatives for immunization, though they may require boosters for sustained protection. Other vaccines encompass subunit, recombinant, and mRNA technologies, which target specific antigens to enhance immune response, offering versatile options for disease prevention in animals.</p></p>
