<p><p>The medical imaging systems market comprises various technologies used for diagnosing and monitoring health conditions. Computed Tomography (CT) scanners provide detailed cross-sectional images. X-ray imaging systems deliver quick, basic views of internal structures. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) systems use magnetic fields for high-resolution images of soft tissues. Ultrasound imaging systems utilize sound waves for real-time visualization, especially in obstetrics. Nuclear imaging systems assess metabolic processes through radioactive tracers, while mammography systems specialize in breast tissue examination for cancer detection.</p></p>
