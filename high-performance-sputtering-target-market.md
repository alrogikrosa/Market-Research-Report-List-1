<p><p>The High Performance Sputtering Target market can be categorized into two main types: Plane Targets and Rotating Targets. Plane Targets are flat surfaces used in various deposition processes, providing uniform coating and are commonly employed in semiconductor and photovoltaic applications. In contrast, Rotating Targets utilize a cylindrical design that allows for continuous material replenishment during sputtering, enhancing efficiency and extending target lifespan. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, with choices influenced by factors like deposition speed, material consumption, and application requirements.</p></p>
