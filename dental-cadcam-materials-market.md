<p><p>The dental CAD/CAM materials market includes various ceramics tailored for restorative dentistry. Glass ceramics offer excellent aesthetics and bond strength, making them ideal for veneers and crowns. Alumina-based ceramics provide enhanced strength and are often used in core structures. Lithium disilicate is renowned for its translucency and durability, suitable for both anterior and posterior restorations. Zirconia is highly durable and is frequently used for frameworks and prosthetics, making it a popular choice in high-stress applications.</p></p>
