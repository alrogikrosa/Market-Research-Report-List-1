H2-receptor antagonists reduce stomach acid by blocking histamine receptors, providing relief from conditions like GERD. PPIs inhibit proton pumps in gastric cells, offering potent acid suppression for peptic ulcers and GERD. The "others" category includes diverse agents, such as antacids and mucosal protective drugs, which complement acid-reducing therapies, addressing various gastric and digestive disorders.</p></p>
