<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging System market is segmented into two primary types: passive and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging relies on detecting naturally emitted terahertz radiation from objects, making it ideal for applications like security screening and material analysis. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves the use of external terahertz sources to illuminate objects, enhancing image contrast and detail. This method is typically used in scientific research and non-destructive testing, providing versatile solutions across various industries.</p></p>
