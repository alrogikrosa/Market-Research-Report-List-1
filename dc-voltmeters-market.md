<p><p>The DC voltmeters market primarily includes chopper type and direct coupled type voltmeters. Chopper type DC voltmeters utilize electronic switching to enhance accuracy and stability, making them ideal for low-level measurements in laboratory settings. In contrast, direct coupled DC voltmeters provide a straightforward measurement of voltage without any intermediate processing, offering high reliability for various industrial applications. Both types cater to different user needs based on precision, application, and measurement range, contributing to a diverse market landscape in the field of electrical measurement.</p></p>
