<p><p>The Air Entrainment Meters market consists of two main types: Water Column Type and Pressure Gauge Type. Water Column Type meters measure air entrainment by assessing the height of water displaced by air bubbles in a column, providing accurate readings for concrete applications. On the other hand, Pressure Gauge Type meters gauge the pressure changes caused by air bubbles in a fluid, offering a quick and efficient method to determine air content. Both types are essential for ensuring quality in construction materials.</p></p>
