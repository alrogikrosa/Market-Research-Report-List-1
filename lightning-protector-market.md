<p><strong>The Lightning Protector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Switch Type</li><li>Pressure Limiting Type</li><li>Combination Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lightning protector market comprises three main types: Voltage Switch Type, Pressure Limiting Type, and Combination Type. Voltage Switch Type devices operate by diverting excess voltage away from protected equipment during a lightning strike. Pressure Limiting Type protect systems by activating under overpressure conditions caused by electrical surges. Combination Type integrates features from both previous types, offering a multifaceted approach to lightning protection. Each type caters to specific needs, enhancing safety and reliability for various electrical systems and installations.</p></p>
