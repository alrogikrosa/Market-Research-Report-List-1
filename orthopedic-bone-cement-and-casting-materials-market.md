<p><p>The orthopedic bone cement and casting materials market primarily includes two types: orthopedic bone cement and orthopedic casting materials. Orthopedic bone cement is used to secure artificial joints and provide stability in orthopedic surgeries, often composed of polymethyl methacrylate (PMMA). On the other hand, orthopedic casting materials are utilized for immobilizing fractured bones, commonly made from fiberglass or plaster of Paris, offering varying levels of rigidity and weight. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring successful recovery and rehabilitation in orthopedic care.</p></p>
