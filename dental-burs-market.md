<p><ul><li>Long Straight Shank (HP)</li><li>Latch-type Shank (RA)</li><li>Friction Grip Shank (FG)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental burs are essential tools in oral healthcare, categorized by their shank types: Long Straight Shank (HP), Latch-type Shank (RA), and Friction Grip Shank (FG). Long Straight Shank burs are used with handpieces for precision cutting. Latch-type shanks secure the bur firmly during procedures and are common in high-speed applications. Friction Grip Shank burs feature a design that allows for a secure fit in handpieces without additional locking mechanisms, providing ease of use and versatility in various dental procedures.</p></p>
