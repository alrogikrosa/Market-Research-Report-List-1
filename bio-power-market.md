<p><p>The bio power market encompasses two primary types: biogas and biomass. In contrast, biomass refers to the direct use of organic materials, such as wood, crop residues, or waste, as fuel for energy generation. Both types contribute to renewable energy production, reducing reliance on fossil fuels while decreasing greenhouse gas emissions and promoting sustainable waste management practices.</p></p>
