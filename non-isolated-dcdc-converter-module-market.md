<p><ul><li>Boost Module</li><li>Buck Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Isolated DC/DC Converter Module market primarily comprises two types: Boost and Buck Modules. Boost Modules increase voltage from a lower to a higher level, making them ideal for applications requiring higher output voltage from a limited input supply. Conversely, Buck Modules decrease voltage from a higher to a lower level, commonly used in battery-powered devices to efficiently regulate voltage levels. Both types are essential for power management in various electronic devices, from consumer electronics to industrial applications, enhancing energy efficiency and performance.</p></p>
