<p><p>The vacuum gas atomizer market features two primary types: single nozzle and multiple nozzles. Single nozzle atomizers employ a single injector for metal powder production, offering simplicity and ease of operation, making them suitable for smaller batches. In contrast, multiple nozzles atomizers utilize several injectors to simultaneously produce larger volumes of powder, enhancing efficiency and productivity. This variety caters to diverse industrial needs, with single nozzles favored for precision and multiple nozzles preferred for higher output demands.</p></p>
