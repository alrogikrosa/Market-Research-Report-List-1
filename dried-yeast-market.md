<p><ul><li>Active Dry Yeast</li><li>Inactive Dry Yeast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dried yeast market is primarily categorized into two types: active dry yeast and inactive dry yeast. Active dry yeast is commonly used in baking, as it remains dormant until activated, promoting fermentation and rising in dough. Inactive dry yeast, on the other hand, is used primarily for nutritional purposes and does not ferment or cause rise; instead, it is rich in proteins and vitamins. Both types cater to different consumer needs in baking and health industries, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
