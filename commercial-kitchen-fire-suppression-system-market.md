The market comprises two main types: Single Agent Fire Suppression Systems utilize a single extinguishing agent, typically foam or dry chemical, designed for specific fire hazards. In contrast, Dual Agent Fire Suppression Systems combine two extinguishing agents, often water and a chemical agent, providing enhanced protection against both grease and other fire types. These systems ensure compliance with safety regulations and enhance overall fire safety in commercial kitchens.</p></p>
