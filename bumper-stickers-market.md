<p><ul><li>Clear</li><li>Opaque</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bumper stickers are classified into clear and opaque market types based on their visibility and design. Clear bumper stickers, often made from transparent materials, allow the vehicle's surface color to show through, enhancing the sticker's aesthetic appeal while delivering messages subtly. In contrast, opaque bumper stickers use solid colors that block the vehicle's surface, offering bold visibility and striking designs. Both types serve different purposes, with clear stickers often favored for subtlety and opaque ones for impactful messaging.</p></p>
