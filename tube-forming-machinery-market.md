Spiral machines are designed for producing helical or spiral tubes, ideal for applications like heat exchangers. Round machines focus on forming cylindrical tubes with consistent diameters, commonly used in piping and structural applications. Oval machines specialize in creating oval-shaped tubes, often utilized in automotive and architectural designs. Each machine type caters to specific requirements, enhancing efficiency and precision in tube fabrication processes.</p></p>
