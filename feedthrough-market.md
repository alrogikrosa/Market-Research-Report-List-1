<p><p>The feedthrough market is categorized into three primary types: electrical, mechanical, and fluid feedthroughs. Electrical feedthroughs facilitate the passage of electrical signals or power across barriers, ensuring efficient connectivity. Mechanical feedthroughs allow mechanical movement or force transmission, often used in applications like robotics. Fluid feedthroughs enable the transfer of gases or liquids, essential in processes requiring sealed environments. Each type serves specific industrial needs, playing a crucial role in applications across sectors such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
