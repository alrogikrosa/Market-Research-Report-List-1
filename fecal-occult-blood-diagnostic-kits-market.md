<p><strong>The Fecal Occult Blood Diagnostic Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunochemical Fecal Occult Blood Test (iFOBT or FIT)</li><li>Guaiac Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fecal occult blood diagnostic kits are used to detect hidden blood in stool samples, aiding in the early diagnosis of colorectal conditions. The market primarily includes two types: Immunochemical Fecal Occult Blood Test (iFOBT or FIT) and Guaiac Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT). iFOBT utilizes antibodies to specifically identify human hemoglobin, offering higher specificity and sensitivity. In contrast, gFOBT relies on chemical reactions to detect blood, but can yield false positives due to dietary factors, making iFOBT increasingly preferred in clinical settings.</p></p>
