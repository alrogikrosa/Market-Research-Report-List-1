<p><strong>The Quartzite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Macrocrystalline Quartzite</li><li>Cryptocrystalline/Microcrystalline Quartzite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quartzite market can be categorized into two main types: macrocrystalline quartzite and cryptocrystalline/microcrystalline quartzite. Macrocrystalline quartzite features larger, visible crystals, making it popular for decorative applications such as countertops and flooring, valued for its aesthetic appeal and durability. In contrast, cryptocrystalline/microcrystalline quartzite consists of finer crystals, providing a smooth texture and often used in products like tiles and aggregates. Both types serve diverse architectural and construction needs, driven by their unique properties and market demand.</p></p>
