<p><p>The portable electric vehicle (EV) charger market is categorized into three types based on charging speed: Slow AC, Fast AC, and Fast DC chargers. Slow AC chargers typically provide low power, taking several hours to fully charge an EV, suitable for overnight home charging. Fast AC chargers deliver higher power for quicker charging times, often used in commercial settings. Fast DC chargers, the fastest option, provide rapid charging, allowing for significant power delivery in a short timeframe, ideal for public charging stations and long-distance travel.</p></p>
