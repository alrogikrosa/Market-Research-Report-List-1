Pore sizes below 0.45µm are crucial for sterilization applications in laboratories and pharmaceuticals, effectively removing bacteria and other contaminants. Membranes with pore sizes ranging from 0.45µm to 6µm are commonly used for filtration in research and diagnostics, allowing for moderate particle retention. Pore sizes above 6µm are suitable for general filtration purposes, including air and water treatment, capturing larger particles and debris.</p></p>
