<p><strong>The Multi functional Infrared Thermometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Infrared Thermometer</li><li>Non-contact Infrared Thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multifunctional infrared thermometer market is primarily divided into two types: contact and non-contact infrared thermometers. Contact infrared thermometers require direct touch with the object's surface to measure temperature, providing accurate readings for solid surfaces. Non-contact infrared thermometers, on the other hand, measure temperature from a distance using infrared radiation, making them ideal for quick assessments, especially in safety-sensitive environments or for measuring the temperature of moving objects. Both types cater to diverse industrial, medical, and consumer applications.</p></p>
