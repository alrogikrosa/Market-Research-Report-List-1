<p><p>EUS-FNA (Endoscopic Ultrasound-Guided Fine Needle Aspiration) is a minimally invasive technique used in diagnosing pancreatic and other abdominal conditions. The market is segmented into two main types: core puncture and edge puncture. Core puncture involves obtaining tissue samples from deeper lesions, providing more substantial biopsies for accurate diagnosis. Edge puncture, on the other hand, focuses on superficial lesions, facilitating faster procedures. Both types are essential in oncology and gastroenterology, contributing to effective disease management and patient outcomes.</p></p>
