<p><p>Motor vehicle insurance encompasses various market types tailored to different coverage needs. Third Party Only insurance covers damages or injuries inflicted on others in an accident for which the policyholder is at fault. Third Party Fire & Theft adds protection against vehicle theft and damages from fire, while still focusing on third-party liabilities. Comprehensive Insurance offers the most extensive coverage, protecting against third-party claims as well as damages to the policyholder's vehicle from accidents, theft, or natural disasters.</p></p>
