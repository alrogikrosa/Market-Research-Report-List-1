<p><p>Mechanical Ventilation Heat Recovery Systems (MVHR) can be categorized into two main types: Whole House Heat Recovery and Single Room Heat Recovery. Whole House Heat Recovery systems are designed to manage ventilation and heat recovery for an entire home, optimizing energy efficiency by recovering heat from exhaust air and redistributing it to incoming fresh air. In contrast, Single Room Heat Recovery systems focus on individual spaces, allowing targeted heat recovery and ventilation in specific areas, ideal for homes with varying occupancy or usage patterns.</p></p>
