The market is segmented into biocide-containing and biocide-free products. Biocide-containing coatings incorporate antimicrobial agents to actively prevent fouling, offering immediate protection but raising environmental concerns. In contrast, biocide-free coatings utilize non-toxic fluoropolymer technology, relying on surface properties to deter fouling without chemicals, appealing to eco-conscious consumers while promoting sustainability and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
