<p><p>The Antimicrobial Silicone Masterbatch market includes two main types: thermoplastic resin and thermosetting resin. Thermoplastic resins are adaptable materials that soften upon heating, allowing for easy processing and reshaping, making them suitable for various applications. Thermosetting resins, on the other hand, undergo a chemical change when cured, resulting in a hard, inflexible structure that maintains its form under heat and pressure. Both types are enhanced with antimicrobial properties to inhibit microbial growth, ensuring safety in consumer products and industrial applications.</p></p>
