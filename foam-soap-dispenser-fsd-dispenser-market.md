<p><p>The Foam Soap Dispenser (FSD) market is categorized into automatic and manual types. Manual dispensers, on the other hand, require users to press a lever or button to release soap. While often more cost-effective, they may pose a higher risk of germ transmission. Both types cater to various settings, emphasizing cleanliness and usability.</p></p>
