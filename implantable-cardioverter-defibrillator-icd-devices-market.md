<p><strong>The Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD) Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Chamber ICD</li><li>Dual-Chamber ICD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD) market features two primary types: Single-Chamber ICDs and Dual-Chamber ICDs. Single-Chamber ICDs consist of one lead and are typically used for patients requiring basic monitoring and defibrillation, primarily for ventricular tachycardia or fibrillation. In contrast, Dual-Chamber ICDs have two leads, allowing more comprehensive monitoring of both the atrium and ventricle, making them suitable for patients with atrial fibrillation or those needing synchronized pacing. These devices cater to varying clinical needs and enhance patient outcomes.</p></p>
