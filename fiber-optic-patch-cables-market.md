<p><ul><li>Singlemode Fiber Optic Patch Cables</li><li>Multimode Fiber Optic Patch Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic patch cable market is primarily divided into two types: Singlemode and Multimode fiber optic patch cables. Singlemode fiber optic patch cables are designed for long-distance data transmission, utilizing a single core to allow light to travel directly. In contrast, Multimode fiber optic patch cables use multiple cores, enabling higher data rates over shorter distances. Each type serves distinct applications, with Singlemode cables suited for telecommunications and high-speed networks, while Multimode cables are commonly used in local area networks and data centers.</p></p>
