<p><p>Leaky feeder systems are specialized communication networks designed for areas like tunnels and underground facilities. They operate primarily in two market types: UHF (Ultra High Frequency) and VHF (Very High Frequency). UHF systems offer higher data rates and better penetration in dense structures, making them ideal for urban environments. VHF systems, on the other hand, are often preferred in more rural or expansive areas, providing longer-range communication with lower frequency signals. Both markets serve essential safety and operational needs in critical environments.</p></p>
